About Us

Founded to Empower Communities
and Drive Change

Founded in 2023, the Future Forward Development Initiative (FFDI) is a youth-led civil society organization committed to empowering marginalized and underserved communities across Nigeria. The organization began at Gombe State University as Start the Future Initiative, where a group of undergraduate students came together with a shared passion for youth development, leadership, and social impact through grassroots action and campus-based advocacy.

Our Mandate

Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

We envision resilient Nigerian communities where empowered young people actively lead climate action, social inclusion, and sustainable development, ensuring every young person has the opportunity to thrive and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

FFDI exists to equip young people and marginalized communities in Nigeria with the knowledge, skills, and support systems needed to overcome social, economic, and environmental challenges, while building sustainable pathways for personal and community transformation.

Our Approach

Guided by integrity and innovation, we prioritize teamwork, excellence, and diversity in fostering a culture of growth, accountability, and sustained impact.

What Drives Us

Building Stronger Institutions

We collaborate with government institutions, civil society, and citizens to deliver improved public services, create access to information, and empower people at every level.

Stronger Communities

We work at the grassroots level to build resilient communities that can respond to social and economic challenges.

Youth Empowerment

We equip young people with leadership skills, knowledge, and opportunities to drive positive change.

Inclusive Development

We promote equity and ensure marginalized voices, including persons with disabilities, are heard and included.

Sustainable Impact

We design solutions that are practical, scalable, and capable of creating long-term change.
